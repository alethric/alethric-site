TacitOps

Reduce Mean Time to Repair.
Give every technician access to the knowledge they need.

TacitOps is an AI guidance system for industrial maintenance and operations, deployed at the asset.

Knowledge capture

The unspoken expertise of your best technicians, structured.

Every intervention, decision, and deviation captured in real time using voice and vision AI, then converted into records that grow more valuable with every session.

Edge deployment

Real-time guidance where connectivity cannot be assumed.

Agentic workflows run at the industrial edge. No cloud dependency. TacitOps operates in the environment that industrial work actually happens in. Online or offline.

Compounding intelligence

Every session makes the system smarter for the next one.

Structured intervention records are indexed into a shared knowledge base. Future sessions surface matched historical context across every technician and every site.

How it works

From intervention
to structured intelligence.

01

Technician initiates a session

A maintenance technician activates TacitOps on a mobile device. Real-time voice and vision capture begins. No manual logging, no forms to fill.

02

AI guides the intervention

The system provides step-by-step guidance, drawing from the knowledge base of past interventions. It flags known failure modes, recommends parts, and warns about risks based on what previous technicians encountered on the same asset.

03

Intervention is captured and structured

Every action and decision is converted into a structured record: asset, failure mode, repair sequence, and outcome. Indexed and searchable across the operation.

04

The next technician starts where the last one finished

Each completed record enriches the shared knowledge base. The more the system is used, the more precisely it can guide the next technician facing the same problem.
